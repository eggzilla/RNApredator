Species - Taxonomic Node with Genomes

Identified by Tax-id
e.g.: 511145 for E.coli K-12 MG1655

often with the rank of "species" or "subspecies" therefore
called "Species Node" in this context.
Clicking the designation of the node will select all
directly associated genomes/plasmids and forward
you to the "Target Search" page.
